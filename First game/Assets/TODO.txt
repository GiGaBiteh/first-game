//Story of the game//
/////////////////////
You're on a mining mission near orbiting jupiter, asteroids have been detected closeby, suddenly, 
two big asteroids crashinto each other, debris of the explosion hits your spaceship and you're spinning violenty, 
the ability of the spaceship to stay into orbit with jupiter fails, you try your best to take control of the spaceship
you were able to crash onto a nearby moon, whilst falling through the atmosphere a team on earth gathered information
about the planet, when you finally landed, you make contact with earth, they ask about you and your possibly existing
crewmate. They tell you to look around and check the damage of the spaceship. After looking, you contact them again
and tell them the engine is broken, and some other things as well. They tell you that those things could be repaired
with the materials hosted by callisto. Turns out it was a mining mission after all.

Undeground you can find the remains of an old civilization
there are underground biomes, and enough different blocks to build your own colony here, you can either choose to
go home or to build your civilization here.

One of the problems of callisto is energy, as it is a cold planet, you'll have to dig deep for geothermal energy.
Solar panels could be an option.
